# Crash Engine Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** May 14, 2026

## Introduction

Crash Engine (“the App”) is developed and published by Code 3 Coders (“we,” “our,” or “us”). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the Crash Engine mobile application.

Crash Engine is designed to assist users with collision documentation, information exchange, photo organization, and structured narrative generation workflows.

## Information We Collect

Crash Engine is designed with a local-first approach.

The App does not require user account creation and does not collect personal information through remote servers.

Information entered into the App may include:

* Collision details
* Vehicle information
* Driver information
* Insurance information
* Narrative notes
* Photographs
* Location-related information entered manually by the user

All information entered into the App is stored locally on the user’s device unless the user chooses to export or share the information.

## Camera and Photo Library Access

Crash Engine may request access to the device camera and photo library in order to:

* Capture collision scene photographs
* Attach images to reports
* Save exported documentation images
* Import existing photos into reports

Photos and media remain on the user’s device unless the user explicitly shares them.

## Data Sharing

Crash Engine does not:

* Sell user data
* Share user data with advertisers
* Upload user data to cloud storage operated by Code 3 Coders
* Use third-party analytics services to collect report contents
* Use AI processing services for user-generated report data

Information may only be shared when initiated directly by the user through:

* Email
* Text message
* Device sharing features
* Exported files

## Data Storage and Security

Crash Engine stores report data locally on the user’s device.

Users are responsible for maintaining the security of their own devices, including:

* Device passcodes
* Biometric authentication
* Device encryption
* Backup settings

While reasonable efforts are made to design the App responsibly, no method of electronic storage or transmission is guaranteed to be completely secure.

## CJIS Considerations

Crash Engine is designed with a local-storage-focused workflow intended to simplify agency review of CJIS-related operational considerations. Agencies and users remain responsible for ensuring their own compliance with applicable policies, procedures, regulations, and legal requirements.

## Third-Party Services

Crash Engine may utilize standard Apple iOS system frameworks necessary for application functionality, including:

* Camera access
* Photo library access
* File sharing
* Email and messaging integration

These services are governed by Apple’s own privacy policies and platform terms.

## Children’s Privacy

Crash Engine is not directed toward children under the age of 13.

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically. Changes become effective when the updated policy is posted.
